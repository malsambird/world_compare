What is this?

This app shows what makes people happy all over the world! Every time you update, you get real-time results!

How does it work?

We search Instagram for the most recent photos with the tag "100happydays" and display the locations as stars on the map. Click the star to see the photo.

What is 100 Happy Days?

100 Happy Days is an initiative that challenges people to take a photo a day of something that makes them happy. Find out more at 100happydays.com.
